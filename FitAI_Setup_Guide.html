What is FitAI?

FitAI is a workout tracker that uses AI to build you a personalized program based on your goals, schedule, equipment, and any injuries. Instead of a generic one-size-fits-all program, you get a program built specifically for you — and it refreshes every few weeks as you get stronger.

1

Add FitAI to your home screen

FitAI works like an app but doesn't require an App Store download. Here's how to install it:

iPhone — Safari or Arc
  1. Open Safari or Arc
  2. Go to the FitAI link you were sent
  3. Tap the Share button at the bottom
  4. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  5. Tap Add — FitAI will appear on your home screen
⚠️ Using Chrome on iPhone? Chrome works fine for browsing FitAI but doesn't support Add to Home Screen on iPhone. Use Safari or Arc to install it on your home screen.
Android
  1. Open Chrome
  2. Go to the FitAI link you were sent
  3. Tap the three dots menu in the top right
  4. Tap Add to Home Screen
2

Create your program with Claude

FitAI works with any AI assistant to build your personalized program. Use whichever one you already have — they all work.

Claude
claude.ai
ChatGPT
chatgpt.com
Grok
grok.com

All three are free to sign up. The prompt is built into the FitAI app — you don't need to write anything. Here's how it works:

This is a conversation, not a form

When you paste the prompt into your AI, it will ask you questions — about your health conditions, injuries, goals, schedule, and fitness history. Just answer honestly and in as much detail as you can. The more your AI understands about you, the better your program will be.

What your AI will ask about
  • • Age, weight, height
  • • Health conditions — blood pressure, heart conditions, diabetes, gout, anything relevant
  • • Current or past injuries and limitations
  • • Medications that might affect exercise
  • • Your real fitness history — what has worked before, what hasn't
  • • Your actual goals beyond just "lose weight"
  • • Your schedule, gym access, and equipment
When you're done answering questions

Once your AI feels it knows enough about you, say "generate my program" — your AI will output your personalized program. Copy the entire response and paste it into FitAI.

Important tips:
  • • Don't rush the conversation — more detail = better program
  • • When copying the response, copy the entire thing — the app finds the program automatically, even if there's extra text
  • • If your AI asks about something you're not sure about, just be honest — it will work around your limitations
3

Load your program into FitAI

  1. 01
    Open FitAI from your home screen
  2. 02
    Tap 📋 Get My Program at the top
  3. 03
    Tap Copy Prompt — then switch to Claude and paste it. Answer Claude's questions in detail.
  4. 04
    When Claude is done asking questions, say "generate my program"
  5. 05
    Copy Claude's entire response, come back to FitAI, paste it in, and tap Load Program
4

Using the app at the gym

Logging a workout

Select your session type (Strength, Cardio, or Mobility) and the correct day. Log your weight and reps for each set as you go. Tap the exercise name to watch a form tutorial video. Hit DONE after each set — the rest timer starts automatically.

Rest timer

The timer opens automatically when you complete a set. Default is 90 seconds. You can change it to 60s, 2min, or 3min. Tap Timer in the top right anytime to open it manually.

Mobility sessions

Switch to Mobility session type. Each stretch has its own Start/Done timer so you can track how long you held each position. The checkbox marks it complete automatically when you tap Done.

Saving & sharing

Tap Save after each workout to save it to your history. Tap Copy & Share to copy your workout log as formatted text — you can paste it into Claude to get feedback or update your program.

5

Refreshing your program

Every 4-6 weeks your program should be refreshed to keep your body adapting. Here's how:

  1. 01
    Use Copy & Share after a few workouts to copy your recent results
  2. 02
    Open Claude and paste your results along with this message: "Here are my recent workout results. Please create a refreshed program for the next 4 weeks and output it as JSON in the same format as before."
  3. 03
    Paste Claude's new JSON into FitAI using the Paste New Program button
